# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

---

## ✅ Actionable Tips

1. Use strong and unique passwords for each account; avoid using the same password everywhere.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps like email, banking, and social media.
3. Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
4. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received through SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
5. Always verify UPI payment requests before approving; check the sender’s name carefully. 💳
6. Do not accept or approve “collect requests” from unknown people on UPI apps. 💰
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.
8. Keep your apps and phone updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
9. Log out from shared or public devices after use.
10. Think before posting personal information on social media.

---

## 🚨 Reporting Cyber Fraud

* Report online fraud at: [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930**

---

## 📌 Tip:

Being alert and informed is the best way to stay safe online. Always double-check before taking any action online.
