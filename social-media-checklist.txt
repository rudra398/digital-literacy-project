# Social Media Checklist for College Students

---

## ✅ Do’s (What You Should Do)

1. Think before you post anything online.
2. Use strong and secure passwords for all accounts.
3. Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely.
4. Keep your personal information private.
5. Verify facts before sharing posts or news.

---

## ❌ Don’ts (What You Should Avoid)

1. Do not share sensitive personal details publicly.
2. Do not engage in online bullying or unnecessary arguments.
3. Do not post inappropriate, offensive, or harmful content.
4. Do not click on unknown links or suspicious messages.
5. Do not spend excessive time on social media that affects your studies.

---

## 📌 Tip:

Responsible social media use helps build a positive digital identity and protects your privacy.
